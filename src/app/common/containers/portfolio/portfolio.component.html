TechSTAR Application Portal

The TechSTAR Application Portal (TAP) is a web application built to streamline the recruitment process for both companies and candidates. A candidate can create a profile including their resume and contact information while their career fair is in session, and upon completing it, recruiters can access and update that profile after or while speaking with the candidate at the career fair. Upon deciding to move forward with a set of applicants, recruiters can easily select these students from a candidate dashboard, create an individual "presence" for each candidate with all of the information and comments collected in their profiles, and notify the candidates of their application status with just one click. This saves the recruiters hours of manual information transfer and eliminates the necessity of sifting through a mountain of paper resumes upon a career fair's end. In addition, recruiters can create and manage multiple sessions of career fairs and filter through candidates by fair, Job ID, school, etc. TAP is written in Angular 5, developed with a node.js backend, and integrated with an internal API dedicated to recruitment at Ultimate Software. This project was created during an internal company-wide hackathon in just 48 hours by a team of interns who wanted to make anxiety-inducing career fairs a far more pleasant experience for recruitment teams and potential interns. We are continuing to improve and maintain this application for future use!
